Headings and Paragraphs

This is a simple HTML page demonstrating the use of headings and paragraphs.

Headings

Headings are defined with the <h1> to <h6> tags. The <h1> tag is the highest level, while the <h6> tag is the lowest level.

This is a Level 3 Heading

Headings help to organize the content and make it easier to read.

Paragraphs

Paragraphs are defined with the <p> tag. They are used to group related content together.
